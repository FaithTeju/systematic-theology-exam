Practical Application

PART C1 — Pastoral Scenarios

15 marks | 5 marks each

Question 38 — (5 marks)

What doctrines are at stake here?

Question 39 — (5 marks)
Scenario: A visiting preacher teaches that believers must keep the Sabbath, follow dietary laws, and celebrate Old Testament festivals in order to be saved. He claims this "new gospel" was revealed to him by an angel.
What doctrines are being violated?

How would you respond to this skeptic?
